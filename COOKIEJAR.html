CookieJar operates as a community fund to incentivise action without governance overhead
Cookie parameters can be period limits (eg cookie per day), amount (eg cookie is 10 HAUS/ 10xDai/.01 ETH) and means of validating access to the Jar
A DAO can fill a CookieJar to enable easy access to -small- funds
The application integrates with V3 DAOs to check if a cookie taker is a DAO member
Smart contracts are easily configurable to support many other validation stratigies (erc20, NFT, Hats, more…)
